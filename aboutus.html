About us


We are from 1st 2nd semester faculty of computing batch group 5.1.This project is based on developing a web application to an online book store.We named it as 'Legion Online Book Store'. The purpose of this project is to provide a user-friendly web interface for book lovers all over the world. This application is targeted towards the online community who enjoy reading books on a regular basis. Through our platform the user will get the opportunity to fulfill their book needs from their homes without the hassle of visiting the local bookstores frequently. Anyone who wishes to buy books should register as a customer by giving the necessary information. They should provide a strong password along with the signing up process and Users can subscribe to get newsletters monthly if they wish to get updated regularly. Through 'Contact Us' option user can give positive or negative comments about the services provided. Customer's opinions ara valued and used as feedback for future improvements. Our website implements a secured payment option and the sensitive information of customers are not exposed under any circumstances. There are many advantages of using our service rather than the traditional method of buying books. It will be a time consuming and tedious task to physically browse through the bookstores for the books we want. But from our site anyone can order the book they want to their doorstep or get an eBook version if available. Also, users get updated frequently about the new arrivals on a monthly basis. Customers can save more money if they order the eBook since no delivery charge will be added to the payment.Customers of any age are allowed to browse through variety of books to choose from our category section. Seasonal offers, discounts and coupons will be given fairly with conditions to registered users who fulfill on annual quota. Users can select multiple books from many languages and genres through this single web app. So, these are some of major facilities that our system provides to its users. Via this web app user get to have hands on experience of an E-commerce online book store. Thank you.
